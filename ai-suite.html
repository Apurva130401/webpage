How does the unified dashboard work?

Our unified dashboard provides a single view of all your AI agents across WhatsApp and voice channels. Monitor performance, manage conversations, and access analytics from one centralized location.

Can I start with one channel and add the other later?

Absolutely! You can start with either WhatsApp automation or voice agents, then seamlessly add the other channel later. Your existing setup and integrations will carry over automatically.

What makes this different from using separate tools?

Unlike separate tools, our unified suite shares integrations, knowledge bases, and analytics across channels. Customers get consistent experiences, and you get simplified management with cross-channel insights.
