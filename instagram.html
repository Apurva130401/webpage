Your Questions, Answered

Is this safe for my Instagram account?

Yes. Our platform uses the official Instagram Graph API and adheres strictly to Instagram's platform policies. We avoid aggressive, spammy behavior and prioritize the long-term health and safety of your account.

What kind of content can the AI create?

Our AI can generate a wide range of text-based content, including captions for your images/videos, ideas for Reels, engaging questions for your stories, and relevant hashtags. It learns your brand voice from your past posts to ensure consistency.

Will the engagement look robotic?

No. Our AI is designed to simulate human-like interaction. It analyzes comment context to provide relevant, non-generic replies. For DMs, it can handle common questions and routes more complex inquiries to you, ensuring authenticity.

How much control do I have?

You have complete control. All AI-generated content can be reviewed, edited, and approved by you before it goes live. You can also define the rules for automated engagement, setting the tone and boundaries for the AI's interactions.
