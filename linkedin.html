Your LinkedIn AI Questions

Is using this tool safe for my LinkedIn account?

Yes. We prioritize account safety above all else. Our tool mimics human behavior, operates within LinkedIn's recommended limits, and uses smart activity delays to ensure your account remains safe and secure.

Do I need a LinkedIn Sales Navigator account?

While not strictly required, we highly recommend using a Sales Navigator account. It provides more advanced search filters and a larger pool of prospects, which allows our AI to deliver much more accurate and effective targeting for your campaigns.

How are the messages personalized?

Our AI analyzes a prospect's profile information, such as their current company, industry, recent posts, and shared connections, to generate unique and relevant connection requests and follow-up messages. This goes far beyond simple [First Name] and [Company] placeholders.
