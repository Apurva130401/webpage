Automotive AI Questions

How does the AI handle test drive scheduling?

The AI can access your dealership's calendar to offer available slots for test drives. It can also collect necessary customer information, like their driver's license, before the appointment.

Can it provide vehicle trade-in estimates?

Yes, the AI can be programmed to ask for key vehicle details (make, model, year, mileage, condition) to provide customers with an initial trade-in estimate, which can then be confirmed by your staff.
