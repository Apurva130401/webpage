Fitness AI Questions

How does the AI handle class bookings with limited spots?

By integrating with your booking software, the AI has real-time access to class availability. It will only offer classes with open spots and can even manage a waitlist if a class is full.

Can we send broadcast messages to all our members?

Yes, you can send broadcast messages to all your members who have opted-in to receive WhatsApp communication. This is perfect for announcing new classes, promotions, or important updates.
