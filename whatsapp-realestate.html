Real Estate AI Questions

How does the AI qualify leads for real estate?

Our AI engages leads with a series of questions to understand their property preferences, budget, timeline, and pre-approval status. It can then score leads and pass the most qualified ones directly to your human agents, saving valuable time.

Can clients schedule property viewings through WhatsApp?

Yes, absolutely. The AI can display your available showing slots (synced with your calendar) and allow clients to book appointments directly within the chat. It can also send automated reminders to reduce no-shows.

How does the AI handle property information requests?

The AI can instantly retrieve and send detailed property information, including descriptions, photos, virtual tour links, and even floor plans, based on client queries. It can also suggest similar properties based on their expressed preferences.
