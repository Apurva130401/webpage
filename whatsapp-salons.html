Salon & Spa AI Questions

Can the AI handle bookings for different services and staff members?

Yes, our AI can be configured to understand your different services and staff availability. It can guide clients to book the right service with the right professional.

How are appointment reminders sent?

You can set up automated reminders to be sent via WhatsApp a specified time before the appointment (e.g., 24 hours). This helps to significantly reduce no-shows.
