Travel & Tourism AI Questions

How does the AI handle complex travel bookings?

The AI can handle a wide range of booking requests. For highly complex or multi-stop itineraries, it can gather all the necessary information from the traveler and seamlessly hand over the conversation to a human agent to finalize the booking.

Can it provide real-time flight status updates?

Yes, by integrating with flight tracking APIs, our AI can provide real-time updates on flight status, delays, and gate changes directly to travelers on WhatsApp.
