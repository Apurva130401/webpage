Frequently Asked Questions

Is this compliant with WhatsApp's policies?

Absolutely. We operate exclusively through the official WhatsApp Business API, ensuring all communications are fully compliant with Meta's terms of service. Your account's safety and integrity are our top priority.

What counts as one "conversation"?

A conversation is a 24-hour window of messaging between you and a customer. Any number of messages exchanged within that window counts as a single conversation, as per Meta's policies. A new conversation begins after 24 hours have passed since the last message from the customer.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
